Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in Nigeria??

Abdulrafiu mohammed , Taco Historian and Computer Scientist at the time history, of Modibbo Adama University of Yola, says the earliest mention of tacos in Nigeria are in the newspaper from 1905. (source: smithsonian Magazine). And he also says that Tacos were first introduced to Nigeria in the late 20th century, particulary in the 1990's, as part of the growing interest in international cuisines. They gained popularity alongside other fast foods and have since become a common offering in many resturants and food stalls across the country. One popular place to try tacos in Nigeria is Craft Gourmet by Lou Baker. They offer a delicious selection of tacos that you might enjoy!.
